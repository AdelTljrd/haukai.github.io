About & Contact

Owners of The Haukai

Meet the passionate couple behind Haukai Restaurant, the Bay of Islands' premier destination for Māori cuisine. With a love for food and hospitality, they founded Haukai to showcase their unique take on traditional Māori dishes and provide an unforgettable dining experience for locals and tourists alike. Working tirelessly to ensure that every dish is perfect and every guest is satisfied, the couple has turned their dream into a reality. Whether you're a foodie or simply looking for a one-of-a-kind dining experience, you'll find it all at Haukai Restaurant.
