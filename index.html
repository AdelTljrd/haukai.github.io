About The Haukai

Welcome to The Haukai Restaurant, a unique dining experience located in the stunning Bay of Islands. Our restaurant specializes in Māori kai, offering a delicious range of dishes that celebrate the rich culture and traditions of New Zealand's indigenous people. Founded by a passionate couple, we are committed to creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere that appeals to both locals and tourists alike. Whether you're looking for a delicious meal, a cultural experience, or a bit of both, The Haukai is the perfect choice. Come join us for an unforgettable dining experience that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied and inspired.
